Heaven moves in circles; earth stands in squares.

At the heart of Chinese metaphysics is a simple principle: the five elemental forces of heaven move in endless circular motion, until they settle upon the earth in stillness and order. Circle Workshop honours this principle — treating timing, environment and destiny as one connected system, BaZi, Qi Men Dun Jia, Feng Shui and date selection, so you can face life's uncertainties with structure, not guesswork.

Philosophy

Those who see clearly never rely on luck.

Circle Workshop was founded on a simple observation: the people who make the best decisions are rarely the ones with the most information. They are the ones who can see clearly through complexity, and act with conviction once they do.

Our work sits at the intersection of classical Eastern analytical methods and modern strategic thinking, applied with the same rigour a financial advisor brings to a balance sheet, or a planning consultant brings to a development site.

“Our work is not to tell you what will happen. It is to help you see what is already true, and decide what to do about it.”

Founder, Circle Workshop
