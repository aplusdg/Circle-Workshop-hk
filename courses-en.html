Courses

Passing on what we know, taught personally.

Private mentorship and small-group classes led personally by our founder, suited to anyone who wants to study BaZi, Qi Men Dun Jia, Feng Shui and related disciplines in a structured way — whether new to the subject or looking to go deeper.

Teaching Philosophy

Why we teach.

The knowledge itself isn't hard to find. What's genuinely scarce is the judgement to apply it well — and that's the reason Circle Workshop offers courses at all. Our aim isn't to compress a whole tradition into a few crash-course sessions, but to walk alongside each student as they build their own way of reasoning through a problem.

Course content is designed and taught personally by our founder, following the same approach that guides our advisory work: depth before breadth, logic before rote formulas. Because every student's background and goals differ, we teach exclusively through private mentorship or small groups — never through a standardised lecture hall format.

“I don't want students to simply memorise the rules. I want them to understand the reasoning behind those rules.”

Founder, Circle Workshop
Course Format

Two formats, tailored to how you learn.

Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to go deep on a specific discipline, we begin with a short conversation to understand your background and goals, then recommend the right format and pace.

I.

Private Mentorship

One-on-one guidance, tailored to the student's level, goals, and schedule. Pacing is fully customised — well suited to those who want a structured, in-depth path, or who want to focus on a specific topic such as their own BaZi chart or a particular Qimen analysis.

II.

Small-Group Classes

Learn alongside fellow students, balancing group discussion with individual attention. Well suited to those who want to build a foundation gradually within an interactive setting, with a more accessible entry point.

Curriculum

From fundamentals to application, step by step.

Content is structured according to the student's level. The topics below are the most common, and the actual pace is adjusted flexibly for private or small-group learning.

01

BaZi Fundamentals & Chart Reading

Starting from the basic logic of the Heavenly Stems, Earthly Branches, and the interplay of the Five Elements, students build the ability to read a chart and translate that reading into practical, usable guidance.

02

Introduction to Qi Men Dun Jia

An introduction to the basic charts and layout logic of Qi Men Dun Jia, with real case studies used to practise applying it to business decisions and strategic analysis in a modern context.

03

Feng Shui Assessment & Practice

Learn the fundamental principles of reading direction, layout, and environmental energy, and through case work and on-site practice, develop the ability to assess residential and commercial spaces.
